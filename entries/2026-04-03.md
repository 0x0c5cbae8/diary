It’s either going to be a spectacular success or a spectacular failure that ends with Facilities calling the Dean. Meanwhile, I'm just here, contemplating the structural integrity of *my* boxspring and whether it can hold up for another quarter of me collapsing onto it after 3 AM PSET sessions. Maybe I should offer my services to Operation: Orbital Dropkick; at least then I'd be doing something productive with the word 'spring' that isn't just 'spring quarter sleep deprivation.'

Anyway, time to try and get some actual rest before the weekend PSETs decide to spontaneously generate themselves on my desk.
